# Physical Existence

## [Ontology](./ontology.md)

The realm of [Existence](./existence.md) that is material and physical. Matter, [energy](./energy.md), spacetime, and all [physical systems](./physical-system.md) reside in Physical Existence.

Physical Existence is the counterpart to [Conceptual Existence](./conceptual-existence.md). Together they span all of [Existence](./existence.md).

## [Axiology](./axiology.md)

Physical Existence is where [entities](./entity.md) manifest in tangible form. Every [physical system](./physical-system.md) — from subatomic particles to galaxies — is an [entity](./entity.md) within Physical Existence.

[Natural Law](./natural-law.md) in Physical Existence governs through physics, chemistry, and biology — the [patterns](./pattern.md) that constrain how matter and [energy](./energy.md) behave.

Physical Existence is often represented by [models](./model.md) that reside in [Conceptual Existence](./conceptual-existence.md). The map is not the territory; the [conceptual system](./conceptual-system.md) is not the physical system it models.

### [Cultural](./culture.md) Definition

> Having to do with the material world as opposed to the spiritual or mental.

<a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/existence" target="_blank">existence (wiktionary)</a>

> Empirical reality; the substance of the physical universe. (Dictionary of Philosophy; 1968)

> In physics, a physical system is a portion of the physical universe chosen for analysis. Everything outside the system is known as the environment.

<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nature" target="_blank">Nature (Wikipedia)</a>

> Nature, in the broadest sense, is the physical world or universe. "Nature" can refer to the phenomena of the physical world, and also to life in general.

### [Pattern](./pattern.md) Expression

Physical Existence contains every material [entity](./entity.md) and physical process across all scales.

#### Cosmic Scale

Galaxies, stars, and planets are [entities](./entity.md) in Physical Existence, governed by gravitational and electromagnetic [patterns](./pattern.md).

#### Human Scale

A [human](./human.md) body is a [physical system](./physical-system.md) within Physical Existence — matter organized by biological [patterns](./pattern.md) into a [conscious](./consciousness.md) [entity](./entity.md).

#### Scoped Physical Existence

As with all [Existence](./existence.md), Physical Existence can be [scoped](./scope.md). Biological Existence is Physical Existence scoped to living systems. Chemical Existence is Physical Existence scoped to molecular interactions.
