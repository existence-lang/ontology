# Judicious

## [Ontology](./ontology.md)

Having, characterized by, or done with good judgment or sound thinking.

## [Axiology](./axiology.md)

Judiciousness matters because sound judgment determines the quality of decisions and actions. An [entity](./entity.md) that applies judicious thinking navigates complexity more effectively, allocating [focus](./focus.md) and [energy](./energy.md) where they produce the greatest benefit.
